Black Bean Tostadas

Cooking for toddlers is impossible. They're unpredictable, they like something one day and hate it the next... it's crazy. You gain weight as a parent because you're always eating two meals. So I just keep trying new stuff, and sometimes, I'm plesantly surprised by their reaction.

Who knew that black beans would be such a hit?!? My daughter specifically asks for these now. There's no hot sauce on theirs, and usually they don't want avocado, but who cares? Cheap protein for days! I got the recipe from Delish.

Ingredients

  • 8 tostadas
  • 2 cups shredded Mexican cheese
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 can refried black beans, or you can make your own

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place tostadas on a large baking sheet and sprinkle evenly with cheese. Bake until cheese is melty, about minutes.
  2. Top tostadas with beans, avocado slices, and hot sauce.
