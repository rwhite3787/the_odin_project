Wiggles History

OG Wiggles

These Wiggles kicked it all off. Anthony, Murray, Jeff, and Greg. Oh, and don't forget Captain Feathersword!

Sam & the Three

Greg had health problems, so Sam took his place. IMO, Sam is the best singer they've had. They did Sam dirty and put Greg back in, leaving Sam high and dry.

Wiggles 2.0

After a few months, all but Anthony retired. Lachy, Simon, and Emma joined. Emma took the Wiggles world by STORM!

Wiggles 3.0

Emma left the Wiggles, and they decided to add another person of each color to increase diversity. Now there are 8 Wiggles! My new fav is Caterina.
